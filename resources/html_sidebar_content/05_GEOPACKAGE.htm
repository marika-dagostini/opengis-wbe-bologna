Consultation

This application enables users to interactively explore satellite-derived land information, geospatial layers, and municipal boundaries in the Bologna metropolitan area. Users may visualize, query, and analyze the data for educational, research, or planning support.

Please note that while the base data comes from authoritative open sources, the final processed layers represent an interpretation specific to this project.
