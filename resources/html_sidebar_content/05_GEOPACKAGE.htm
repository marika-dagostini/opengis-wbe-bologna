Consultation

This application enables users to interactively explore satellite-derived demographic information, geospatial layers, and municipal boundaries in the Bologna metropolitan area. Users may visualize, query, and analyze the data for educational, research, or planning support.

Please note that while the base data comes from authoritative sources, the final processed layers represent an interpretation specific to this project.

Opendata

  • Municipality of Bologna boundaries: Sourced from ISTAT (Italian National Institute of Statistics), open use permitted for non-commercial and research purposes.
  • Domiciled Population Estimates: Derived by adjusting official 2022 resident population data from ISTAT using a correction factor based on the Regional Assistance Registry.
  • Demographic Information: Sourced from the ISTAT database.
  • Population Health and COVID-19 Case Data: Provided by the Local Health Authority of Bologna.
  • Wastewater Surveillance Data: Collected through the SARI project (Epidemiological Surveillance for SARS-CoV-2 in Urban Sewage in Italy), coordinated by the National Institute for Public Health (ISS). Accessed via a dedicated data-sharing platform.
